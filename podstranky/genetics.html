Diagnostics of genetic polymorphisms
using the Real-Time PCR method

InnovaDx ®  genetic kits are designed for the determination of genetic polymorphisms from clinical samples causing various genetic diseases. The detection is performed using the Real- Time PCR method with melting curve analysis.

Developed techniques allow minimizing the number of required steps and guarantee maximum result stability. Diagnostic kits  InnovaDx ®  are provided in the form of lyophilized reaction mixtures and are intended to work with the most commonly used Real-Time instruments.

  • Complex approach to PCR diagnostics includes sample preparation (DNA/RNA extraction), Real-Time PCR, and result evaluation
  • Lyophilized Ready Master Mix for the detection of DNA/RNA in real-time
  • Unifom PCR protocols for the detection of a wide range of genetic polymorphisms
  • Noncompetitive Internal Control
  • Storage of all kit components at the same temperature (2–8)°С for 12 months; no freezing required
  • Transportation at the temperature up to 26 °С for no more than 10 days is acceptable
  • Compliance with the requirements of EN ISO 13485:2016Compliance with the requirements of EN ISO 13485:2016Compliance with the requirements of EN ISO 13485:2016
Format of InnovaDx ® -Genetics kits:
