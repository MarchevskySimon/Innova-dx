Diagnostics of genetic polymorphisms
using the Real-Time PCR method

InnovaDx ®  diagnostic kits are designed for the detection of nucleic acids of microbiological agents isolated from clinical samples, causing various infectious diseases. The detection is carried out using the Real-Time PCR method with hybridization-fluorescence TaqMan probes. Developed techniques allow minimizing the number of required steps and guarantee maximum result stability. Diagnostic kits InnovaDx ®  are provided in the form of lyophilized reaction mixtures and are intended to work with the most commonly used Real-Time instruments.

  • Complex approach to PCR diagnostics includes sample preparation (DNA/RNA extraction), Real-Time PCR, and result evaluation
  • Lyophilized Ready Master Mix for the detection of DNA/RNA in real-time
  • Compatible PCR and RT-PCR protocols for the detection of a wide range of pathogens
  • Noncompetitive Internal Control
  • Storage of all kit components at the same temperature (2–8)°С for up to 12-18 months; no freezing required
  • Transportation at the temperature up to 26 °С for no more than 10 days is acceptable / up to 20°C in case of TwoStep version
  • Compliance with the requirements of EN ISO 13485:2016
Formats of InnovaDx ® OneStep kits:
Str

Lyophilized ready-to-use reaction mixture in white strips in a 96-well plate (8 x 0.2 ml tubes), to which extracted DNA/RNA samples are added.

Uni

Lyophilized ready-to-use reaction mixture in clear 0.2 ml tubes, to which extracted DNA/RNA is added, one tube equals one reaction.

  • All necessary components for the PCR reaction are part of the lyophilized reaction mixture. In the case of PCR with reverse transcription (RT-PCR), a separate reverse transcription step is not required.
  • To set up the PCR reaction, it is sufficient to only add samples of extracted DNA/RNA and initiate PCR or RT-PCR.

InnovaDx ® TwoStep kits format:

Lyophilized Master Mix in 0.2 ml 8-tube strips, add diluent and extracted DNA/RNA into the tubes.

Real-time PCR instrument compatibility with InnovaDx ® TwoStep kits:

CFX96™ (Bio-Rad, USA) or other Real-Time PCR systems with FAM, R6G, ROX, Cy5 channels (or their spectral analogues) with similar spectral characteristics.
